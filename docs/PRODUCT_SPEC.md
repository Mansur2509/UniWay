# Product Specification

## Purpose

EduVerse helps school students and applicants make structured, evidence-aware progress across admissions, exams, events, activities, research, careers, and financial literacy. Uzbekistan is the initial market, followed by Central Asia and broader international expansion.

## Product principles

- Academic usefulness before novelty.
- Clear evidence and official sources before speculative predictions.
- Student agency before automation.
- AI assists decisions and learning; it does not replace authorship or judgment.
- Core discovery tools remain useful on the free plan.
- Important information is dated, sourced, and accompanied by a verification reminder.

## Initial users and roles

- **Student:** owns a profile, explores universities and events, builds a roadmap, practices exams, and uses limited AI and essay feedback.
- **Organizer:** submits and manages events, then tracks moderation.
- **Admin:** moderates events and content, manages users and source records, and inspects audit logs.

## Free-plan commitment

The free plan permanently includes the university database, Event Map, basic roadmap, basic profile, basic finance lessons, SAT and IELTS question banks, and limited AI mentor and essay feedback access.

## Subscription model

Phase 0 defines mock tiers: Free, $5, $10, and $25. Real payment providers are out of scope. Access is represented by a plan, counters, limits, and a monthly reset boundary.

## Guardrails

### Admissions

Do not show exact invented probabilities. Compare a profile against official published data using: Below Published Range, Near Published Range, Within Published Range, Above Published Range, or Data Unavailable.

Required disclaimer:

> EduVerse does not guarantee admission. Comparisons are based on publicly available data and are for informational purposes only. Always verify important information with official university sources.

### Essays

Provide feedback, questions, structure guidance, rubric assessment, and revision direction. Do not produce a submission-ready application essay or replace the student's voice.

### Finance

Teach concepts such as budgeting, saving, inflation, debt, risk, and investing basics. Do not give personalized financial, investment, tax, legal, trading, or crypto advice.

### Exam content

Use public exam specifications and create original lessons, questions, answers, and explanations. Proprietary question banks must not be copied.

### Events

Organizer-submitted events require moderation. Users must be directed to verify deadlines, eligibility, location, price, and rules with the organizer.

## Phase 0 acceptance criteria

- Repository rules and product memory are documented.
- Frontend and backend have runnable skeletons.
- Placeholder product routes communicate the intended direction.
- Core database boundaries, API contracts, security controls, and deployment options are documented.
- Phase 1 is divided into independently implementable tasks.
